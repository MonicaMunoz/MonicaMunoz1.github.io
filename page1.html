Bowser Vs Mario


The images are from the classic Super Mario games. I made the first one from text edit and the second one is made from audacity. they both have that distortion quality. They relate to each other because they are in a constant struggle/battle with each other. Mario is in his long conquest to save the princess (Princess Peach) from The evil King Koopa(Bowser).
