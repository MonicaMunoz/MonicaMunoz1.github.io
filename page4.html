For this 3D project, the object of cultural significance I chose was Boo from Super Mario. To me, this represents Game culture. Since this game is very popular and is a classic, I thought it would be a good idea to make a 3D model of it. Most people in the gaming community can agree that Super Mario was a part of their childhood and is a game they always come back to.
