This Minecraft project is based on the character Boo from the classic Super Mario games. I chose to do it on this ghost enemy because It is considered to be part of game culture. Most people in the gaming community know of this cherished character. I grew up playing the Super Mario games franchise with my brothers and completely adore this figure.
